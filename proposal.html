Project Description

What is the topic of your project?

I want to create a portfolio website for my career as a student, and to showcase the skills that I have to offer.

Why did you choose this topic?

I feel that this is a pertinent topic for me since I'm getting to a point where I'd like to get internships, as well as part time jobs. I want to show my skills to potential employers.

What background knowledge do you bring to this topic?

I used to own a portofolio website a couple of years ago when I was showcasing my skills as a calligraphy artist, and though I didn't know any CSS or HTML then, I am familiar with the kind of look and feel a site like that should have. I am also an expert in myself, so I would not run out of information or knowledge to put on the site.

Who is your intended audience?

I would like for potential employers, as well as my professors to see my website. This will highlight my skills to them.

Timeline

Unfortunately I don't have a clear idea for a timeline yet.
